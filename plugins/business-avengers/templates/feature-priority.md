## Prioritization Criteria

### Scoring Model

Each feature is scored on a scale of 1-10 for:

1. **Business Value**
   - Revenue impact
   - Customer retention
   - Market differentiation
   - Strategic alignment

2. **User Impact**
   - Number of users affected
   - Frequency of use
   - Pain point severity
   - User satisfaction improvement

3. **Technical Feasibility**
   - Development effort
   - Technical complexity
   - Risk level
   - Dependencies

4. **Time Sensitivity**
   - Market timing
   - Competitive pressure
   - Regulatory requirements
   - Customer commitments

**Priority Score Formula:**
```
Priority = (Business Value × 0.3) + (User Impact × 0.3) +
           (10 - Technical Complexity × 0.2) + (Time Sensitivity × 0.2)
```
